About

This idea started as an independent study during my senior year at Michigan State University. Throughout my four years, I had used countless learning management systems and all seemed to be missing something. The user experience was poor and the universal accessibility was non-existent. This astonished me as thousands of higher education institutions were using systems that were hard to use and inaccessible to students with a variety of disabilities or learning impairments.

Axcess looks to challenge these current offerings of learning management systems currently in place by thousands of educational institutions throughout the country. By focusing on student engagement and ensuring the technology is accessible to all, Axcess will hopefully soon become the standard for how educational content should be delivered digitally.

Currently, learning management systems fail to adhere to student and faculty needs. Many platforms lack features such as metrics on student engagement or muddy their features in cluttered navigations that are hard to use by most students. Axcess fixes this issue by incorporating user insight from the beginning.

This website will be constantly updated to allow prospective users the chance to provide their feedback on the most up-to-date version of the design or production available. Removing the secrecy so often found in development allows Axcess to be a system for all. Be sure to come back often for all of the updates.

Features

  • Analytics on student engagement
  • Discussion boards
  • Customizable course templates
  • Automatic video transcripts
  • Grade report with calculated final grade
  • Course activity log
  • List of things due each week
  • Fully accessible according to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines
